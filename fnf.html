Description

13th Friday Night: Funk Blood is a Friday Night Funkin mod inspired by the Jason Friday the 13th (1980) horror movie.

Boyfriend and Girlfriend have decided to join up few campers for a camping trip, while hanging out by themselves they decided to have some fun, but unknown to them they have entered the forbidden territory and their presence has reawakened the dormant killer.

So make sure to always download the PC version if you have a PC.

If you want a challenge, turn off Ghost Tapping in the settings.
